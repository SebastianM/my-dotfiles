T480 vs T440p

T480 ports 
- 2 usb 3.1 Gen 1 (one always on) 
- 1 usb 3.1 type-c (power/DP)
- 1 usb 3.1 type-c gen 2/Thunderbolt 3 (power/DP)
- HDMI 1.4b
- Ethernet (RJ-45)
- nano-SIM card slot (WWAN models)
- 4-in-1 media reader (MMC, SD, SDHC, SDXC)
- side docking connecter
- security keyhole
-

T440p ports
- 2 usb 3.0 (one always on)
- VGA
- Mini DP
- Ethernet (RJ-45)
- 4-in-1 media reader
- dock connector (integ only)
-
